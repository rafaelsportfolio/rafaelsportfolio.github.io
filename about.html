About Me

Hi! My Name is Rafael and I am a self taught photographer. I do photography for the creativity, looking at subjects, coming up with a story, or maybe show a reflection of myself through the lense of my camera. Growing up, I've always had some trouble connecting with people around me. I have found that for me, capturing photos can do some of the talking, knowing that people look at my pictures helps me feel connected in some odd way. A sense of familiarity and nostalgia, a space of calm and peace is what I strive for when taking pictures. It's my passion, showing people the art in what they see in their everyday lives.
